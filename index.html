FAQs

What is Showmax?

Showmax is a streaming service that offers you a wide variety of award-winning TV shows, movies, documentaries, Showmax Originals, kids shows, sport and more. You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want without a single advert. There's always something new to discover and shows and movies are added weekly. There no contracts and you can cancel at any time. Get Showmax all for one low monthly price. Showmax offers two plans: a standard Showmax entertainment plan and a Showmax Pro plan. Showmax Pro gives you access to all the entertainment you love plus live sport from SuperSport, news and more.

What devices can I watch on?

This depends on which plan you choose and what suits you best.

If you choose the standard Showmax or the Showmax Pro plan - you can watch on a number of devices such as smartphones, tablets, laptop, smart TV, gaming consoles and media boxes at the same time.

If you choose the cheaper Showmax mobile plan - you can watch on either your smartphone or tablet. Of course you can upgrade at any time should you choose to do so, the choice is yours.

Note: Live streaming services may not be supported on certain devices such as older gaming consoles or smart TVs.

Do I need Wifi or data?

Yes. You need a connection with a minimum speed of 5Mbps. You can use Wi-Fi and/or data, it’s up to you. Inside the Showmax mobile app you are able to control how much data you use by choosing from 3 streaming qualities.

Tip: To save costs, download using free Wi-Fi (for example at coffee shops, malls or salons) but please be responsible and adhere to current social distancing regulations.

What is streaming and downloading?

Streaming means watching videos using the Internet and requires data (Wi-Fi or mobile data). Downloading a show on your mobile or tablet allows you to watch offline. This will save the content to your phone or tablet so you can watch it anywhere, anytime.
